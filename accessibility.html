Accessibility Statement

Our Commitment to Accessibility
TRIBAL is committed to making its website accessible and user-friendly for all visitors, regardless of ability. We invest time, expertise, and creativity to improve accessibility across our digital platforms.

Our website follows the World Wide Web Consortium’s (W3C) Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 AA, supporting users who are blind, have cognitive disabilities, visual impairments, or other accessibility needs.

We provide an accessibility interface that allows visitors to customize the website’s user interface and design based on their individual preferences and requirements.

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. We regularly review, audit, and test our website to ensure our web properties meet or exceed accessibility standards.

Limitations & Ongoing Improvements

While we strive for full accessibility, some content may not yet be fully accessible due to third-party integrations, embedded iframes, or technical limitations beyond our direct control. Where this occurs, we aim to clearly identify limitations and offer reasonable alternatives when possible.

We continuously enhance our accessibility tools by updating features and functionality in line with technological advancements and internal research.

Feedback & Support

If you experience difficulty accessing any part of the website or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us at Support@tribaliii.com.

We actively work to achieve and maintain compliance with WCAG 2.1 AA standards, although not all content may be fully accessible at all times.
